# The Legend of Zelda (NES) - Creature List & Visual Descriptions

This document contains a complete list of all enemies and bosses found in the original *The Legend of Zelda* for the NES, along with visual descriptions to assist in sprite creation.

## Overworld Enemies

### Octorok
*   **Type:** Red / Blue
*   **Description:** Octopus-like creature that walks on land. Round body with a snout.
*   **Visuals:**
    *   **Red:** Red body, white eyes/snout.
    *   **Blue:** Blue body, white eyes/snout.
    *   **Action:** Moves in 4 cardinal directions, shoots rocks from snout.

### Tektite
*   **Type:** Red / Blue
*   **Description:** Spider-like creature with one large eye and two long, thin legs.
*   **Visuals:**
    *   **Red:** Red body, white eye.
    *   **Blue:** Blue body, white eye.
    *   **Action:** Hops around erratically.

### Leever
*   **Type:** Red / Blue
*   **Description:** Conical creature with spinning spikes/teeth on the outside. Resembles a sea anemone emerging from the sand.
*   **Visuals:**
    *   **Red:** Red body, spinning animation.
    *   **Blue:** Blue body, spinning animation.
    *   **Action:** Burrows out of the ground, spins, and dives back in.

### Peahat
*   **Type:** Standard
*   **Description:** Plant-like creature with a large spinning flower propeller on top.
*   **Visuals:** Yellow/Orange bulbous bottom, large spinning green/white propeller on top.
*   **Action:** Flies around, stops to rest (vulnerable only when resting).

### Moblin
*   **Type:** Red / Blue
*   **Description:** Bulldog-faced goblin warrior holding a spear.
*   **Visuals:**
    *   **Red:** Red skin, white/green tunic.
    *   **Blue:** Blue skin, white/red tunic.
    *   **Action:** Walks around, throws spears.

### Lynel
*   **Type:** Red / Blue
*   **Description:** Centaur-like beast (half-man, half-lion) with a sword and shield.
*   **Visuals:**
    *   **Red:** Red body, white mane/shield.
    *   **Blue:** Blue body, white mane/shield.
    *   **Action:** Aggressive, shoots sword beams.

### Armos
*   **Type:** Standard
*   **Description:** Animated stone statue soldier.
*   **Visuals:** Orange/Brown armor, round shield, helmet. Looks like a statue until touched.
*   **Action:** Moves quickly when touched.

### Ghini
*   **Type:** Standard
*   **Description:** One-eyed ghost.
*   **Visuals:** White, wispy body, single large eye, long tongue.
*   **Action:** Floats through walls, usually found in graveyards.

### Zola (Zora)
*   **Type:** Standard
*   **Description:** Aquatic creature that pops out of the water.
*   **Visuals:** Green/Teal head, fins on side of head.
*   **Action:** Surfaces from water, shoots a fireball, submerges.

### Rock (Boulder)
*   **Type:** Standard
*   **Description:** Falling rocks from Death Mountain.
*   **Visuals:** Brown, round, craggy rock texture.
*   **Action:** Bounces down the screen.

## Dungeon Enemies

### Zol & Gel
*   **Zol (Big Slime):**
    *   **Visuals:** Green/Gray blob, slightly pointed top, two eyes.
    *   **Action:** Splits into two Gels when hit.
*   **Gel (Small Slime):**
    *   **Visuals:** Small, round, teal blob.
    *   **Action:** Moves erratically, attaches to player.

### Rope
*   **Type:** Standard / Flashing
*   **Description:** Snake.
*   **Visuals:** Brown/Green snake body, distinct head.
*   **Action:** Moves slowly, but charges quickly when aligned with the player.

### Keese
*   **Type:** Standard (Blue) / Red / Black
*   **Description:** Bat.
*   **Visuals:** Simple bat silhouette with flapping wings.
    *   **Blue:** Standard dungeon bat.
    *   **Red:** Varies by palette.
    *   **Black:** Varies by palette.
*   **Action:** Flies over obstacles, rests occasionally.

### Stalfos
*   **Type:** Standard
*   **Description:** Skeleton warrior with two swords.
*   **Visuals:** White skeletal body, skull head, holding two swords.
*   **Action:** Walks around, sometimes shoots beams (in later quests/hacks, usually just walks in vanilla).

### Wallmaster
*   **Type:** Standard
*   **Description:** Giant disembodied hand.
*   **Visuals:** Orange/Red hand with long fingers.
*   **Action:** Emerges from the wall/floor to grab the player and take them back to the dungeon entrance.

### Goriya
*   **Type:** Red / Blue
*   **Description:** Dog/Rat-like warrior that uses a boomerang.
*   **Visuals:**
    *   **Red:** Red fur/tunic.
    *   **Blue:** Blue fur/tunic.
    *   **Action:** Throws a boomerang.

### Wizzrobe
*   **Type:** Red / Blue
*   **Description:** Wizard in a robe.
*   **Visuals:**
    *   **Red:** Red robe, pointed hat, white face (shadowy).
    *   **Blue:** Blue robe, pointed hat.
    *   **Action:**
        *   **Red:** Teleports, shoots magic beam.
        *   **Blue:** Phases in and out, walks through blocks, shoots magic beam.

### Darknut
*   **Type:** Red / Blue
*   **Description:** Heavily armored knight with a large shield and sword.
*   **Visuals:**
    *   **Red:** Red armor, large shield covering front.
    *   **Blue:** Blue armor.
    *   **Action:** Walks randomly, immune to frontal attacks (must hit back or side).

### Pols Voice
*   **Type:** Standard
*   **Description:** Rabbit/Ghost-like creature with big ears and whiskers.
*   **Visuals:** Cream/Orange color, large ears, hopping animation.
*   **Action:** Hops around, weak to arrows (and sound in Famicom version).

### Like Like
*   **Type:** Standard
*   **Description:** Tube-like creature (looks like a stack of pancakes or a shield).
*   **Visuals:** Orange/Yellow cylindrical body, pulsating.
*   **Action:** Moves to engulf the player, eats their magical shield.

### Gibdo
*   **Type:** Standard
*   **Description:** Mummy.
*   **Visuals:** Bandaged body, looks similar to Stalfos but "fleshy" and wrapped.
*   **Action:** Walks slowly, takes many hits.

### Moldorm
*   **Type:** Standard
*   **Description:** Segmented worm.
*   **Visuals:** Series of round segments (circles) linked together. Red/Orange.
*   **Action:** Moves in erratic curves. Shrinks as segments are destroyed.

### Vire
*   **Type:** Standard
*   **Description:** Devil/Gargoyle-like creature.
*   **Visuals:** Blue body, wings, two horns.
*   **Action:** Hops around. Splits into two red Keese when hit.

### Bubble
*   **Type:** Red / Blue / Flashing
*   **Description:** Floating skull surrounded by flashing bubbles/flames.
*   **Visuals:** Skull center, surrounded by a ring of colored lights.
*   **Action:** Bounces off walls. Contact disables sword (Red/Flashing) or permanently jinxes (Blue - only in Second Quest).

## Bosses

### Aquamentus (Level 1, 7)
*   **Description:** Unicorn Dragon.
*   **Visuals:** Green body, single horn on head, small wings.
*   **Action:** Stationary/Paces slightly, shoots 3 fireballs, vulnerable head.

### Dodongo (Level 2)
*   **Description:** Triceratops-like dinosaur.
*   **Visuals:** Yellow/Green tough skin, three horns.
*   **Action:** Walks slowly. Impervious to sword. Must be fed bombs to damage.

### Manhandla (Level 3)
*   **Description:** Four-headed piranha plant monster.
*   **Visuals:** Central core with four long limbs, each ending in a large open mouth (clamp). Green/Orange.
*   **Action:** Moves faster as heads are destroyed. Shoots fireballs.

### Gleeok (Level 4, 8)
*   **Description:** Multi-headed dragon (2, 3, or 4 heads).
*   **Visuals:** Large body, long necks, dragon heads with horns. Green/Red palette.
*   **Action:** Heads shoot fireballs. When a head is cut off, it flies around independently.

### Digdogger (Level 5)
*   **Description:** Giant sea urchin / eye.
*   **Visuals:** Large orange sphere with a single eye.
*   **Action:** Indestructible until the Whistle is played, which shrinks it into a small, vulnerable version.

### Gohma (Level 6)
*   **Type:** Red / Blue
*   **Description:** Giant crab/spider cyclops.
*   **Visuals:** Hard shell, single large eye (eyelid opens and closes), legs.
    *   **Red:** Red shell.
    *   **Blue:** Blue shell.
*   **Action:** Moves side to side, shoots fireballs. Vulnerable only when eye is open (requires arrow).

### Ganon (Level 9 - Final Boss)
*   **Description:** The Prince of Darkness. Pig-like demon.
*   **Visuals:** Large blue/green pig monster, holding a trident. Often invisible.
*   **Action:** Teleports, shoots fireballs, requires Silver Arrow to kill.

## Traps & Hazards

### Blade Trap
*   **Visuals:** Blue/Metal spiked block.
*   **Action:** Slides quickly towards player when they cross its path.

### Stone Statue
*   **Visuals:** Dragon/Gargoyle head statue.
*   **Action:** Shoots fireballs at the player.
